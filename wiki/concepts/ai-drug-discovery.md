# AI Drug Discovery

AI drug discovery refers to the use of machine-learning models to accelerate the identification, design, and optimization of candidate therapeutics. It spans tasks from target selection and hit finding through lead optimization and preclinical characterization. Specific techniques include generative models for small molecules and proteins, structure prediction, and ML-guided screening pipelines.

## Why it's an active commercial space

Pharma R&D is expensive and slow; even modest efficiency gains translate to large dollar impact. That has drawn both dedicated biotech-AI startups and general-purpose AI labs into the space.
