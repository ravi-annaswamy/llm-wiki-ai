# AI Drug Discovery

> **Using ML models to accelerate target selection, hit finding, lead optimization, and preclinical characterization of therapeutics.** Pharma R&D is expensive and slow; even modest efficiency gains translate to large dollar impact — which is why both dedicated biotech-AI startups and general-purpose AI labs are entering the space.
