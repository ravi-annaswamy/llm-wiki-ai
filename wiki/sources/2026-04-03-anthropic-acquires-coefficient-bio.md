# Anthropic acquires Coefficient Bio in $400M deal

> **[anthropic](../entities/anthropic.md) has acquired [coefficient-bio](../entities/coefficient-bio.md) — a ~10-person stealth biotech AI startup founded ~8 months earlier — in a ~$400M all-stock deal.** Signal: Anthropic is building a vertical life-sciences capability with dedicated research talent, not just positioning Claude as a general-purpose tool for scientists.

**Original:** TechCrunch, by Dominic-Madori Davis, 2026-04-03. Reporting sourced from The Information and Eric Newcomer; TechCrunch confirmed the close but not the price.

## Key facts

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Deal size | ~$400M, all-stock (per The Information / Newcomer; unconfirmed by TechCrunch) |
| Target | Coefficient Bio, stealth, ~8 months old |
| Founders | [samuel-stanton](../entities/samuel-stanton.md), [nathan-c-frey](../entities/nathan-c-frey.md) — both ex-[prescient-design](../entities/prescient-design.md) (Genentech) |
| Headcount | ~10, joining Anthropic's health & life sciences team |
| Strategic context | Follows Oct 2025 launch of [claude-for-life-sciences](../entities/claude-for-life-sciences.md) |

## Why it matters

Extends Anthropic's healthcare push from "general-purpose tool for scientists" to first-party domain expertise shaping life-sciences products. Acquiring a team with hands-on [ai-drug-discovery](../concepts/ai-drug-discovery.md) experience is a vertical-integration move, not a platform play.
