# Anthropic acquires Coefficient Bio in $400M deal

**Original:** TechCrunch, by Dominic-Madori Davis, published 2026-04-03. Reporting sourced from The Information and Eric Newcomer; TechCrunch independently confirmed the deal closed but not the price.

## Summary

[anthropic](../entities/anthropic.md) has acquired [coefficient-bio](../entities/coefficient-bio.md), a stealth biotech AI startup, in a roughly $400M all-stock deal. Coefficient Bio was founded about eight months before the acquisition by [samuel-stanton](../entities/samuel-stanton.md) and [nathan-c-frey](../entities/nathan-c-frey.md), both formerly of [prescient-design](../entities/prescient-design.md) (Genentech's computational drug discovery group). The ~10-person team is expected to join Anthropic's health and life sciences group.

The acquisition extends Anthropic's strategic push into healthcare that began publicly with the October 2025 launch of [claude-for-life-sciences](../entities/claude-for-life-sciences.md), a product aimed at helping scientific researchers accelerate discoveries. Coefficient Bio was applying AI to make [ai-drug-discovery](../concepts/ai-drug-discovery.md) and broader biological research more efficient.

## Key facts

- **Deal size:** ~$400M, structured as stock (per The Information / Newcomer; TechCrunch could not confirm the amount).
- **Target:** Coefficient Bio, stealth, ~8 months old at time of sale.
- **Founders:** Samuel Stanton, Nathan C. Frey — both ex-Genentech Prescient Design.
- **Headcount:** ~10 people, moving into Anthropic's health & life sciences team.
- **Strategic context:** Follows October 2025 launch of Claude for Life Sciences.

## Why it matters

This is a signal that Anthropic is building a vertical life-sciences capability with dedicated research talent, not just positioning Claude as a general-purpose tool for scientists. Acquiring a team with hands-on drug-discovery experience suggests the company wants first-party domain expertise shaping its life-sciences products.

## Open questions

- What specific techniques was Coefficient Bio using — protein design models, generative chemistry, or workflow-level automation?
- How will the team be integrated: product team for Claude for Life Sciences, a research arm, or both?
- Is this the first of a series of vertical acqui-hires, or a one-off bet on this specific team?
