### Generative model theory (foundational)
- [score-based-generative-models](concepts/score-based-generative-models.md) — Hub concept. Parameterize ∇ₓ log p(x), train by score matching, sample by Langevin / reverse-SDE. Unified with diffusion models under the SDE framework.
- [score-matching](concepts/score-matching.md) — Training objective for fitting a score network from samples alone; denoising score matching is the practical workhorse.
- [langevin-dynamics](concepts/langevin-dynamics.md) — MCMC sampler that needs only the score; annealed variant chains multiple noise scales to fix high-dimensional failure.
- [diffusion-models](concepts/diffusion-models.md) — Denoising diffusion perspective; equivalent to score-based modeling under the SDE unification (wave-vs-matrix-mechanics analogy).
- [probability-flow-ode](concepts/probability-flow-ode.md) — Deterministic ODE with the same marginals as the SDE; unlocks exact log-likelihoods and deterministic sampling.
