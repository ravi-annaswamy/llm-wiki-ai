# Google DeepMind

> **Google's flagship AI research lab; owns the vertical stack for LLM-driven algorithm discovery** — Gemini 2.5 Pro (mutation operator), OpenSpiel (evaluation substrate), AlphaEvolve (distributed evolutionary system).

The CFR and PSRO results (VAD-CFR matching SoTA on 10/11 games; SHOR-PSRO on 8/11) are one payload of a broader bet: **algorithm research becomes a search problem with a well-defined fitness signal.** See [llm-driven-algorithm-discovery](../concepts/llm-driven-algorithm-discovery.md).

DeepMind's vertical ownership is itself an [own-your-substrate](../analyses/own-your-substrate.md) instance — the compounding asset is the discovery infrastructure, not any single discovered algorithm.
