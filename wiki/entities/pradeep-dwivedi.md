# Pradeep Dwivedi

Group CEO of [[entities/eros-media-world]]. He is the on-record voice behind Eros's plan to review its full ~3,000-title catalog for AI-assisted adaptations — extending the experiment that began with the "Raanjhanaa" re-release (Source: [[sources/2026-04-04-reuters-ai-indian-films]]).

Dwivedi framed the strategy to Reuters as "both a revenue opportunity and a creative renewal strategy," explicitly tying AI re-editing to the studio's financial pressure (Eros International's Indian unit saw annual revenue from operations fall 44% last year). See [[concepts/ai-film-re-editing]].
