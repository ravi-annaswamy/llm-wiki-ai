# Stefano Ermon

> **Stanford CS professor and Yang Song's PhD advisor.** Co-author on every paper in Song's 2019–2022 sequence that built and unified score-based generative models with diffusion.

Co-authorship includes: NCSN (NeurIPS 2019), NCSNv2 (NeurIPS 2020), the ICLR 2021 SDE paper (Outstanding Paper Award) that unified score-based modeling with [diffusion-models](../concepts/diffusion-models.md), the NeurIPS 2021 Spotlight on maximum-likelihood training, and the ICLR 2022 medical-imaging inverse-problems paper. His Stanford group is one of the principal research centers for the score-based / diffusion model family.
