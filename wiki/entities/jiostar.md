# JioStar

Indian media joint venture between billionaire Mukesh Ambani's Reliance and Walt Disney. In the context of this wiki, JioStar is notable as the distribution home for the first AI-generated episodic series to emerge from [collective-artists-network](collective-artists-network.md)'s Galleri5 lab: an adaptation of the Hindu epic Mahabharat (Source: [2026-04-04-reuters-ai-indian-films](../sources/2026-04-04-reuters-ai-indian-films.md)).

The series has recorded at least 26.5 million views since its October release on JioStar's streaming platform. For scale comparison, an earlier non-AI TV adaptation of the Mahabharat drew 200 million viewers between 1988 and 1990.

Alok Jain, a senior JioStar executive, told Reuters the audience response "has been a mix of appreciation and healthy debate, which is natural for any ambitious creative leap," and said JioStar is exploring making original stories in AI format — a signal that commercial distributors, not just production studios, are betting on AI-native content.
