# JioStar

> **Reliance/Disney streaming joint venture; distribution home of the Galleri5 AI Mahabharat series.** 26.5M+ views since October 2025. Commercial distributors, not just studios, are betting on AI-native content.

For scale: the 1988–1990 non-AI TV Mahabharat drew ~200 million viewers. Senior executive Alok Jain told Reuters the audience response "has been a mix of appreciation and healthy debate, which is natural for any ambitious creative leap," and said JioStar is exploring original AI-format stories.
