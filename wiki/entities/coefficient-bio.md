# Coefficient Bio

> **Stealth biotech AI startup acquired by Anthropic in April 2026 for a reported $400M all-stock.** ~10 people, ~8 months old, no publicly disclosed IP. Founded by two ex-Prescient Design computational drug discovery researchers.

| Dimension | Detail |
|---|---|
| Founded | ~August 2025 |
| Founders | [samuel-stanton](samuel-stanton.md), [nathan-c-frey](nathan-c-frey.md) |
| Prior employer | [prescient-design](prescient-design.md) (Genentech) |
| Headcount | ~10 |
| Acquirer | [anthropic](anthropic.md) |
| Deal | ~$400M all-stock (The Information / Newcomer; TechCrunch confirmed close, not price) |
| Focus | AI applied to [ai-drug-discovery](../concepts/ai-drug-discovery.md); specifics undisclosed |

The team is expected to join Anthropic's health and life sciences group, extending work that began publicly with [claude-for-life-sciences](claude-for-life-sciences.md).
