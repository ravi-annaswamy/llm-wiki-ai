# Coefficient Bio

Stealth biotech AI startup, acquired by [[entities/anthropic]] in April 2026 in a reported $400M all-stock deal (Source: [[sources/2026-04-03-anthropic-acquires-coefficient-bio]]).

## Background

- **Founded:** ~August 2025 (roughly 8 months before the acquisition).
- **Founders:** [[entities/samuel-stanton]] and [[entities/nathan-c-frey]], who previously worked together in computational drug discovery at [[entities/prescient-design]], Genentech's in-house AI drug-design group.
- **Team size:** ~10 people at time of acquisition.
- **Focus:** Applying AI to make [[concepts/ai-drug-discovery]] and other forms of biological research more efficient. Specific techniques and products were not publicly disclosed — the company operated in stealth.

## Outcome

The team is expected to join Anthropic's health and life sciences group, extending the work that began publicly with [[entities/claude-for-life-sciences]]. The acquisition price was reported by The Information and Eric Newcomer; TechCrunch confirmed the deal closed but not the amount.
