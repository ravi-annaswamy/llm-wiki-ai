# Coefficient Bio

Stealth biotech AI startup, acquired by [anthropic](anthropic.md) in April 2026 in a reported $400M all-stock deal (Source: [2026-04-03-anthropic-acquires-coefficient-bio](../sources/2026-04-03-anthropic-acquires-coefficient-bio.md)).

## Background

- **Founded:** ~August 2025 (roughly 8 months before the acquisition).
- **Founders:** [samuel-stanton](samuel-stanton.md) and [nathan-c-frey](nathan-c-frey.md), who previously worked together in computational drug discovery at [prescient-design](prescient-design.md), Genentech's in-house AI drug-design group.
- **Team size:** ~10 people at time of acquisition.
- **Focus:** Applying AI to make [ai-drug-discovery](../concepts/ai-drug-discovery.md) and other forms of biological research more efficient. Specific techniques and products were not publicly disclosed — the company operated in stealth.

## Outcome

The team is expected to join Anthropic's health and life sciences group, extending the work that began publicly with [claude-for-life-sciences](claude-for-life-sciences.md). The acquisition price was reported by The Information and Eric Newcomer; TechCrunch confirmed the deal closed but not the amount.
