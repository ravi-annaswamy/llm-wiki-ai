# Abundantia Entertainment

> **Bollywood production house targeting one-third of revenue from AI-generated or AI-assisted content within three years.** $11M AI studio, built from scratch rather than retrofitted.

Founded by Vikram Malhotra. Malhotra's statement to Reuters frames the greenfield approach as a deliberate contrast with catalog-driven plays ([eros-media-world](eros-media-world.md)) and talent-agency-driven plays ([collective-artists-network](collective-artists-network.md)'s Galleri5). A useful data point on how traditional production houses are capitalizing the shift to [ai-filmmaking-india](../concepts/ai-filmmaking-india.md).
