# Abundantia Entertainment

Bollywood production house founded by Vikram Malhotra. Notable in this wiki for a concrete AI capital commitment: Abundantia recently announced an $11 million AI studio build and, per Malhotra's statement to Reuters, expects AI-generated or AI-assisted content to account for **one-third of its revenue within three years** (Source: [[sources/2026-04-04-reuters-ai-indian-films]]).

Malhotra said Abundantia is building its AI capability from scratch rather than retrofitting existing workflows — a notable contrast with catalog-driven plays like [[entities/eros-media-world]]'s, and with talent-agency-driven plays like [[entities/collective-artists-network]]'s Galleri5. Useful as a data point on how traditional production houses are capitalizing the shift to [[concepts/ai-filmmaking-india]].
