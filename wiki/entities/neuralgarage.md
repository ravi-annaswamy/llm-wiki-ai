# NeuralGarage

> **Bengaluru AI dubbing startup serving Yash Raj Films and other major studios.** Alters faces to match new audio while preserving the performer's identity — AI solving a pre-existing grievance (mismatched lip-sync) rather than replacing live performance.

Co-founder Subhabrata Debnath demonstrated the system to Reuters: an English-speaking AI character given a German track appeared to speak fluent German with lip and jaw in sync within minutes. Used on Yash Raj Films' *War 2* (Hindi → Telugu).

Dubbing is plausibly the lowest-friction entry point for AI in Indian film: 22 official languages and hundreds of dialects make dubbing a baseline requirement for any national blockbuster.
