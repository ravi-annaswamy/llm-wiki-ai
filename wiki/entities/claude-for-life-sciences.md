# Claude for Life Sciences

Anthropic product announced in October 2025, aimed at helping scientific researchers make discoveries (Source: [[sources/2026-04-03-anthropic-acquires-coefficient-bio]]).

It is the public face of [[entities/anthropic]]'s push into healthcare and life sciences. The April 2026 acquisition of [[entities/coefficient-bio]] — bringing in a team with hands-on [[concepts/ai-drug-discovery]] experience from [[entities/prescient-design]] — appears to extend this effort with dedicated domain talent rather than just general-purpose model access.
