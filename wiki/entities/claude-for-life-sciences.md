# Claude for Life Sciences

Anthropic product announced in October 2025, aimed at helping scientific researchers make discoveries (Source: [2026-04-03-anthropic-acquires-coefficient-bio](../sources/2026-04-03-anthropic-acquires-coefficient-bio.md)).

It is the public face of [anthropic](anthropic.md)'s push into healthcare and life sciences. The April 2026 acquisition of [coefficient-bio](coefficient-bio.md) — bringing in a team with hands-on [ai-drug-discovery](../concepts/ai-drug-discovery.md) experience from [prescient-design](prescient-design.md) — appears to extend this effort with dedicated domain talent rather than just general-purpose model access.
